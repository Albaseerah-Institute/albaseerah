📖 Course Objectives

The worship of Allah is an obligation upon every Muslim, and this worship cannot be fulfilled correctly except through proper knowledge. Without understanding who Allah is, how He commands us to worship Him, and what He expects from us, a person risks falling into error. This is why Albaseerah Institute is dedicated to teaching you the fundamentals of Islam, ensuring that your faith and practice are built upon authentic knowledge.

Whether you are a new Muslim seeking clarity, a teenager eager to deepen your understanding, or someone who simply desires to strengthen their connection with Allah, this course provides the essential foundation needed to worship Allah with sincerity, correctness, and devotion.

Through structured lessons on Aqeedah (Islamic beliefs), Fiqh (acts of worship), Seerah (life of the Prophet ﷺ), Adab (manners), and Tarbiyah (spiritual development), you will gain the knowledge, confidence, and spiritual discipline necessary to practice Islam as it was revealed—free from confusion and cultural distortions.

At Albaseerah, our mission is not just to teach but to nurture true understanding, awaken hearts, and help you live Islam as a way of life—so that your worship becomes meaningful, accepted, and a source of success in this life and the Hereafter.
